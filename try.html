Dear Liam,

As you prepare to return home to England, I find myself overwhelmed with emotions that are difficult to put into words. Meeting you online was a twist of fate, one that led to a beautiful, albeit brief, chapter in my life. From our first conversations, I felt a connection, but nothing could have prepared me for the joy and warmth I felt when I picked you up at the airport. I was so nervous, but seeing you for the first time after our two weeks of talking online melted all my worries away.

Our time together was nothing short of magical. From trying Filipino foods to exploring famous landmarks, every moment was a new adventure. When you went to Boracay, your photos and videos of the stunning beaches made me yearn to experience it with you. And then you did something so incredible – you booked a flight for me to join you. That first airplane ride of mine led to some of the most unforgettable days of my life.

Swimming at the beach, sharing ice creams like Mango Mama, Coco Mama, and Halomango by the shore at night, are memories I will treasure forever. Our newfound love for cheesy oysters, which I promise to learn how to make perfectly, the party boat, and snorkeling – each moment was filled with joy, laughter, and the kind of happiness that only comes from being with someone who truly cares.

In just two weeks, you became such a significant part of my life. You helped me overcome my fears, and you made me feel cherished in a way I never thought possible. You are the nicest, sweetest, most loving, and caring man I've ever known. Your kindness and generosity have left a lasting mark on my heart.

As you travel back home, know that I will take care of the watermelon floatie and your favorite jacket, keeping them as cherished reminders of our time together. I wish you all the best in your future travels and hope you meet wonderful new friends, because you have a heart of gold.

I hope you come back one day so we can continue our adventures and see the world together. Our story started at the airport, and I hope it doesn’t end there. The last time I saw you at the airport, I cried so much, but those tears were filled with gratitude for the beautiful moments we shared.

I miss you all the time, and I hope this isn’t just an “airport love story.” You’ve become such a special part of my life, and I’ll always hold on to the hope that our paths will cross again.

P.S

I wasn't crying while writing this.

With all my love,

Patricia
